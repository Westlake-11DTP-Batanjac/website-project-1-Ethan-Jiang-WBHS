Twitter Facts

  • #1 Twitter was founded in March, 2006.
  • #2 Twitter has more than 330 million monthly active users.
  • #3 The most followed Twitter account is @BarackObama, with more than 130 million followers.
  • #4 Twitter's bird logo is named "Larry the Bird" after basketball player Larry Bird.
  • #5 The first tweet was sent by Jack Dorsey, co-founder of Twitter, on March 21, 2006. It read "just setting up my twttr".
